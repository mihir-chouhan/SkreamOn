About KJSCE

Set in the heart of the city, K. J. Somaiya College of Engineering is located in the Somaiya Vidyavihar Campus. Catering to about 40,000 students every day, this campus offers diverse fields of education such as Engineering, Management, Commerce, Arts and Sciences. Along with numerous institutes, Somaiya Vidyavihar also boasts of a campus covering 65 acres.

About Skream

KJSCE Skream -A National Level Sports Festival started off 3 years ago with the sole purpose of putting passion and spirit of sports on the platform they deserve. We bring to you the fourth edition of the four day sporting extravaganza, Skream 2017. Hosting a varied set of events, colossal crowds and eminent personalities, Skream 2017 has a plethora of new competitive opportunities in store for you. Over the years, Skream has grown exponentially and is bigger than ever before. So, let's come and Skream together.
