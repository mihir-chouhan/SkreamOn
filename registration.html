STEPS TO FOLLOW :

Step 1: Download the Registration Form and Bonafide Certificate.
Step 2: Fill out the two forms and attach the team list given with the Registration Form.
Step 3: Send scanned copies of the Registration Form, Bonafide Certificate and Team List to kjsceskream@somaiya.edu
Step 4: Continue to payment for your respective event. Payment can be made through the following modes:-

Online
Click on the “Pay Now” button on the respective page event and fill out the details.

Offline
Payments can be made via demand draft in the name of Students’ Association of K.J.Somaiya College of Engineering. Scanned copy of the same s
hould be sent at kjsceskream@somaiya.edu .The demand draft should be submitted on the arrival to the respective event head.
The original copies of the mentioned documents must be brought along with the team for the competition and have to be submitted at the Registration Desk upon arrival.
